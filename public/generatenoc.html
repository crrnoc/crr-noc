About NOC Certificate

No Objection Certificate (NOC) is an official document issued by the institution to certify that:

  • All academic requirements have been fulfilled
  • No dues are pending from the student
  • Library books have been returned
  • No disciplinary actions are pending
  • Student is eligible for the requested clearance

Important Information

Please ensure all your fees are paid and library books are returned before generating your NOC. The certificate will be automatically generated based on your current academic and financial status.
